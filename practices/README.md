# AI Best Practices & Management

This directory contains cross-cutting concerns, management strategies, and general best practices for integrating AI into the engineering workflow.

## Topics
- **Prompt Engineering**: Techniques for getting the best results from LLMs.
- **Context Management**: How to manage context windows and provide relevant information.
- **Ethics & Security**: Data privacy, bias mitigation, and secure AI usage.
- **Team Management**: How to manage AI-augmented teams and measure productivity.
- **Tool Selection**: Criteria for selecting and evaluating AI tools.
